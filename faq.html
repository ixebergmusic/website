How Does the Order Process Work?

Step 1: You fill out an order form from your artist dashboard.

Step 2: We will email you an invoice.

Step 3: Once the invoice is paid, we'll get started on your project.

Step 4: You can track the progress of your order on your artist dashboard.

What Payment Methods Are Accepted?

We accept Cash App, PayPal, and bank transfers. Details will be provided on the invoice.

How Long Does It Take to Complete an Order?

The time frame depends on the complexity of your project. Estimated delivery times will be provided during the order process.

Can I Make Changes to My Order?

Yes, you can request changes during the review phase. Additional fees may apply for significant changes after work has started.

Is There a Refund Policy?

Refunds are available only under specific circumstances. Please review our refund policy or contact support for more details.
