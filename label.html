Who We Are

ixe berg music is an innovative music label dedicated to empowering artists by providing them with the tools and resources they need to succeed in the music industry.

Royalties Policy

Unlike traditional music labels, ixe berg music collects 0% royalties from our signed artists. This means you retain complete ownership of your music and its earnings.

Free Promotion

By signing with ixe berg music, you gain access to our free promotion services. We will promote your tracks across our growing network of social media platforms to help you reach a wider audience.

Why Choose Us?

At ixe berg music, we prioritize your artistic vision and career growth. Our unique approach to artist support ensures that you keep your profits while benefiting from professional promotional services.

Artist Support Services

We offer personalized support tailored to your specific needs, including:

  • Custom beat production
  • Mixing and mastering services
  • Professional music video production
Our team is here to help you achieve your creative goals.

Collaboration Opportunities

As part of ixe berg music, you'll have the chance to collaborate with talented artists and producers within our network. These opportunities can help you expand your creative horizons and build lasting professional relationships.

Our Mission

Our mission is to redefine what it means to be part of a music label. By eliminating royalties and focusing on artist empowerment, we aim to create a platform where musicians can thrive creatively and financially.

How to Join

Interested in becoming part of ixe berg music? Here's how:

  • Submit your application through our support email - support@ixebergmusic.com
  • Provide samples of your work
  • Discuss your goals with our team during a consultation
We're always on the lookout for passionate, talented artists to join our growing family.
